OUR COLLEGE CLUBs

Natraja

Nataraja, the dancing society of YMCA University of Science and Technology, Faridabad was established around 15 years back. The club aims at providing a platform for the engineering students to showcase their love of dance, thereby helping them in honing their talents/hobbies with the help of proper rehearsals, dance activities and various inter/intra college competitions. Nataraja consists of two sub-sections which represent different genres of dancing. These are:
WESTERN: It includes the western dancing styles like hip-hop, freestyle, popping, locking, belly dancing and so on.
FOLK: It includes various folk dances of Indian states like bhangra, giddha, haryanvi along with Indian classical dances like kathak and bharatnatyam.

Srijan:

Srijan club is a Fine Arts oriented club for the Students interested in arts of YMCA University of Science & Technology, Faridabad. The club was established in January, 2003. This club aims at the wholesome development of the engineering students through organization of number of art workshops and developing the skills and enhancing the creativity.

Ananya:

Ananya, the debating and literary club of YMCA University of Science and Technology was founded around 15 years back by a passionate group of people. The club was initially named as Abhivyakti, but the name was changed to Ananya, the unparalleled in 2006. The club aims at the development of communication, writing, public speaking and stage managerial skills of the students through various activities, practice sessions and inter/intra college competitions.
