ABOUT OUR HOSTEL




Facilities in Boy's Hostel

(1) One reading room in each boys’ and girls’ hostel with newspapers and magazines.
(2) Shift canteen in Girls hostel.
(3) One common room in girls’ hostel and two common rooms in boys’ hostel with LED TVs.
(4) Table Tennis room in boys’ hostel.
(5) Wifi facility available in all hostels.
(6) All hostels have independent warden for proper administration and discipline.
(7) Water coolers fitted with RO are provided.
(8) All hostels are under the surveillance of CCTV cameras and security guards.
(9) Separate officers’ mess for staff and guests of staff & students.
(10) The hostels are provided with carom board, chessboard, and skipping rope for recreation & entertainment of the residents within hostel premises.
(11) Guest room in girls’ hostel for female relatives of students.



Facilities in girl Hostel

(1)Reading room, common room, Guest room, Waiting lounge, gymnasium
2) Badminton court and basket ball ground along with open spaces attached to each wing
3) Shift canteen and tuc shop
4) Induction cooktops, gysers,RO water purifiers,CCTV cameras.
5) Wi-Fi
6) Sports and Recreation facility
7) First Aid and Ambulance
6) Separate wardens for each wing,lady guards,resident attendant
